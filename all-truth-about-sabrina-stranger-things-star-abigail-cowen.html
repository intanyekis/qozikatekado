Who is Abigail Cowen?

Abigail F. Cowen was born on 18 March 1998 so under the zodiac sign of Pisces. in Gainesville, Florida USA. She is a 22-year-old actress, probably best recognized for landing the role of Mia Tanner in the CBS drama series “Wisdom Of The Crowd” (2017-2018), playing Dorcas in the Netflix supernatural horror series “Chilling Adventures Of Sabrina” (2018-2020), and featuring as Bloom Peters in the Netflix teen drama series “Fate: The Winx Saga” (2021).

How rich is she, as of now? Abigail Cowen Net Worth

As of early 2021, Abigail Cowen’s net worth is over $500,000, acquired through her successful involvement as a professional actress, working on 10 television and film titles since she launched her career in the mid-2010s.

Early Life, Family, Nationality, Ethnicity, Religion

Abigail Cowen spent her early years on a farm near her hometown of Gainesville. There she was raised by her father, Andrew Cowen, and her mother, Leslie LeMasters-Cowen, with her brother named Dawson Cowen. He graduated with a BS degree in Health Science from the University of Florida in 2019, and currently works as a Critical Care Technician at UF Health. Abigail holds American nationality and belongs to White Caucasian ethnic group, and is a Christian.

Educational Background

Abigail developed her love for acting very early; while in fourth grade, she started taking acting classes, then went to Oviedo High School, and while there competed in track and field.

Upon matriculation, she enrolled into the University of Florida, from which she graduated with a BA degree in Public Relations in 2016.

Career Beginnings

Following her graduation, Abigail Cowen moved with her family to Los Angeles, California to pursue her career as a professional actress. According to her IMDB profile, she made her debut appearance in the role of Brooklyn in the episode “There’s No Place Like Homecoming” of the Fox teen medical comedy-drama series “Red Band Society” in 2014.

Her breakthrough role came three years later, when she was chosen to play Vicki Charmichael in two episodes – “Chapter One: MADMAX” and “Chapter Three: The Pollywog” – of the Netflix sci-fi horror mystery-thriller series “Stranger Things”, created by the Duffer Brothers, her performance in which opened up more opportunities for her, and led her to win more major roles.

Rise to Prominence

In 2017, she joined the cast of the television drama series “Wisdom Of The Crowd”, created by Ted Humphrey, and based on the Israeli series of the same title.

She starred in the recurring role of Mia Tanner until the series ended in the following year. She was then cast to portray Eliza Hunter in the family drama series “The Fosters”, aired on the ABC Family network in 2018.

Continued Success

Later in 2018, Abigail Cowen took the recurring role of Dorcas in the Netflix supernatural horror series “Chilling Adventures Of Sabrina”, broadcast on Netflix until 2020. The series is developed by Roberto Aguirre-Sacasa, and based on the Archie comic book series of the same title. While starring in the series, Abigail even performed two songs – “Always Is Always Forever” and “The Song of Purple Summer”.

In 2019, she was cast as Ricochet in the comedy streaming mini-series “The Power Couple”, along with Alexa and Carlos PenaVega. In the same year, she appeared in the music video for the song “Holding Together” by TOMOS, featuring Ana Michell.

Recent Years

In 2020, Abigail Cowen finally made her debut film appearance in the role of Adrienne in the Erwin brothers’ Christian romantic drama film “I Still Believe” (2020), which follows the life of American contemporary Christian music singer-songwriter Jeremy Camp.

Most recently, she gained worldwide recognition after starring in the main role of Bloom Peters, a 16-year-old fire fairy raised on Earth by humans, in the Netflix teen drama series “Fate: The Winx Saga” (2021), along with Hannah van der Westhuysen and Precious Mustapha. It represents the live-action adaptation of the Italian-American animated series “Winx Saga”, broadcast on RAI channels in Italy and Nickelodeon.

Upcoming Projects

Regarding her upcoming projects, it’s announced that Abigail Cowen will be featured as Fiona in Elle Callahan’s fantasy horror thriller film “Witch Hunt”, next to Elizabeth Mitchell and Christian Camargo.

It’s currently under post-production, and set to be released in March 2021. Besides that, she will play Angel in the upcoming Christian romantic drama film “Redeeming Love”, directed by D. J. Caruso, which is also under post-production.

Other Ventures

Besides that, she sings and plays several instruments, including guitar.
