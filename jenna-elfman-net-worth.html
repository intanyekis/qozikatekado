Jenna Elfman is a television and film actress with an estimated net worth of $16 million. She has gained most of her fame and wealth from playing a role in the sitcom named “Dharma and Greg”. She was born in Los Angeles, California on September 30, 1971.

Jenna Elfman is said to have first made her television appearance in the music video “Halo” as a dancer. She made a number of appearances on various music videos like Anthrax's song Black Lodge. She also performed as a dancer in the music video “Somebody's Crying” by Chris Isaak in 1995.

She was a known face in television commercials. She made guest appearances in two episodes of “Two and a Half Men”, an episode of “Brothers and Sisters”, and also in one of the episodes of “My Name is Earl”. She also starred in “Accidentally on Purpose”, and the CBS comedy “Courting Alex”. Jenna has appeared as the supporting actress in the movie, Friends with Benefits. She also appeared in the 2013 comedy TV series “1600 Penn”. She walked the ramp in the Red Dress Collection Fashion Show. With such a long list of credits to her name, the million dollars of assets is well justified.

Elfman met her husband Bodhi Elfman at a Sprite commercial and is married to him since 1995. She is blessed with two sons. She is also into social work and works as a criminal rehabilitation activist too.
