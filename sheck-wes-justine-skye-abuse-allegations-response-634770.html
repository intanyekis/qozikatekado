Sheck Wes Responds To Justine Skye's Abuse Allegations

August 2024 · 3 minute read

The back and forth between rapper Sheck Wes and singer Justine Skye regarding the former’s abuse allegations has escalated. Earlier today, the “Mo Bamba” rapper denied the accusations made by Skye having to do with a video she posted on her Twitter account earlier this morning. The video showed the rapper hopping the fence to the “Build” singer’s home and yelling her name. The rapper took to his Twitter to deny the components of the tweet.

“Just clarifying this is a video of me hopping a fence to get my stuff back from a girl who refused to give me my stuff,” he wrote.

Just clarifying this is a video of me hopping a fence to get my stuff back from a girl who refused to give me my stuff . Once again for the record I never hit or beat up any woman .

— Sheck Jesus (@sheckwes) February 12, 2019

Justine has since replied to Wes’ tweet providing more information of Wes’ alleged abuse. “Did you forget about the time you launched my phone across the room at the Montrose hotel, slapped me in the face with money saying “get a new one bi**h,” the singer started off the tweet.

Did you forget about the time you launched my phone across the room at the Montrose hotel, slapped me in the face with money saying “get a new one bitch”, the two hotel security guards holding you back from attacking me while I ran away. The cops coming… https://t.co/c1Z5vZitG3

— Justine Skye (@JustineSkye) February 12, 2019

The back and forth between the two did not stop there. Wes responded just moments later: “This is a complete lie. I called my hotel to come get you away from me because I stayed there a lot and I was a young artist scared and afraid of a girl like you.”

This is a complete lie . I called my hotel to come get you away from me because i stayed there a lot and i was a young artist scared and afraid of a girl like you . All this back and forth is childish , people talk and resolve or go to police not provoke social media ,

— Sheck Jesus (@sheckwes) February 12, 2019

The two artists have been going back and forth on the social media platform since late Monday night when Skye tweeted “taking a walk with my friends and my man and Sheck Wes (my abuser) and his friends decide to STALK us and attack my friends.”

Taking a walk with my friends and my man and Sheck Wes (my abuser) and his friends decide to STALK US and attack my friends.. two cars full of niggas while he sat in the car like a bitch. You’re pathetic sheck and you beat women. You hit your girl before me and you’ll do it again

— Justine Skye (@JustineSkye) February 12, 2019
