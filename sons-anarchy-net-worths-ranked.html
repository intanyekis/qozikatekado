Sons of Anarchy is one of the most popular television shows of all time, following Jax Teller, who is involved in a motorcycle club that has to deal with life as a father and an outlaw all at the same time.

Trying to deal in both legal and illegal business all while uncovering buried secrets about his past. The show lasted for seven seasons and received numerous awards, remaining incredibly popular to this day through Netflix.

With a large cast of characters, Sons of Anarchy had some incredibly talented actors and actresses work on it over the years, and within this article, we will rank the cast in terms of their net worth.

Tommy Flanagan (Net Worth: $3 Million)

Tommy Flanagan is a Scottish actor who has appeared in a variety of shows and movies such as Braveheart, Alien Vs Predator, and many more, although he is best known for his work as Chibs on Sons of Anarchy.

With a net worth of $3 million, the show allowed Flanagan to really showcase his talents as one of the most important characters on the show, Chibs slowly gets trusted with increasing power and responsibility as it goes on.

As a trusted advisor to Jax Teller, Chibs is one of the most loyal characters on the show, which quickly made him a fan favorite amongst viewers.

Ryan Hurst (Net Worth: $4 Million)

Ryan Hurst plays a beloved character in the form of Opie on Sons of Anarchy, with his death being one of the shows most emotional moments that fans simply didn't expect to see until it happened.

Opie is Jax Teller's best friend on the show and remains loyal to his brother right until the bitter end, despite the fact that it is through his association with the gang that his wife ends up being murdered.

As well as his appearances on Sons of Anarchy, Hurst has gained his $4 million net worth through roles on King and Maxwell, L.A. Cops, and movies like Saving Private Ryan.

Theo Rossi (Net Worth: $4 Million)

Theo Rossi is another Sons of Anarchy actor who has a net worth of $4 million, which is largely down to his role as "Juice," who is one of the most popular characters throughout the entire series.

Despite being incredibly loyal, Juice begins to have suicidal and depressing thoughts, which was an interesting storyline despite the fact he was in a tough group, and because of that, the rest of the club begin to doubt him.

Kim Coates (Net Worth: $4 Million)

Another one of the most popular characters in Sons of Anarchy is Tig, who is the Sergeant-At-Arms for the motorcycle club and is often tasked with going to kill various people who the club deems a threat.

This job ends up causing Tig some serious mental problems as he is put through some incredibly difficult emotional moments, such as watching his own daughter be killed, which makes him one of the most interesting characters on the show.

Mark Boone Junior (Net Worth: $5 Million)

Mark Boone Junior is certainly best known for his role as Bobby Munson on Sons of Anarchy, although he has gained his net worth of $5 million through a variety of roles such as being in Memento, Batman Begins, and Die Hard.

Within Sons of Anarchy, Bobby is one of the most trusted and highly respected members of the club, as a former Vice-President, although he ends up resigning due to not being happy with the actions of Jax.

Bobby is known for providing the truth, no matter whether the person wants to hear it, and he ends up meeting a brutal end, being tortured and then killed for his betrayal.

Maggie Siff (Net Worth: $8 Million)

Maggie Siff is a terrific actress who has a key role in Sons of Anarchy, portraying one of the lead roles in the show despite the fact she isn't ever a member of the gang itself, instead, her character Tara ends up in a relationship with Jax Teller.

Away from the motorcycling world, Siff has been involved in another major television series in Mad Man, as well as movies like Funny People to earn her net worth of $8 million.

Kurt Sutter (Net Worth: $10 Million)

Kurt Sutter plays the character of Otto in Sons of Anarchy, who is already in prison by the time we meet him, yet remains incredibly loyal to the club, often putting himself through serious punishment to help from inside the jail.

However, it isn't through that small part that Sutter really contributes to the show, as even though he plays an absolute maniac on-screen, in reality, he is actually the man who wrote and directed the entire show.

That's right, Sons of Anarchy is the brainchild of Sutter, which is where his $10 million net worth mainly comes from, with the fans having him to thank for everything.

Ron Perlman (Net Worth: $15 Million)

Heading into the start of Sons of Anarchy, Ron Perlman was one of the most recognisable names on the show, having already had a very well established career in the acting world, being best known for his role as Hellboy.

Perlman portrays Clay Morrow in Sons of Anarchy, who is one of the main characters on the show which has one of the more interesting storylines throughout, being an original member of the gang, he does whatever it takes in order to stay on top.

Perlman does an incredible job on the show, at times being incredibly likeable and on the flipside being someone you want to see get what he has coming to him. With a net worth of $15 million, largely down to this show, Perlman is a name who will never be short of work.

Charlie Hunnam (Net Worth: $16 Million)

No matter what else Charlie Hunnam does in his career, he is always going to be best known for his role on Sons of Anarchy, where he portrays the shows main character, Jax Teller.

That isn't a bad thing either, because the show was so popular and successful, with Charlie Hunnam being one of the main reasons that things kept going as the fans were so invested in his character and what he was doing.

Katey Sagal (Net Worth: $30 Million)

She might not play a member of the Sons of Anarchy gang, but Katey Sagal's character,  Gemma Teller Morrow, who is the mother of Jax and the wife of Clay, is one of the most important characters on the entire show.

Often the emotional anchor trying to keep things together, Gemma is put through a lot by her family and is someone that they all try to protect at all costs, despite the fact she has no problems looking after herself.

As well as acting on the show, Sagal also sings in the background, providing some of the music for the series. With a net worth of $30 million and a lengthy career in the industry, she is one of the most successful members of the cast.
