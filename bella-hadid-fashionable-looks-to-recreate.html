10 Fashionable Bella Hadid Outfits To Recreate

July 2024 · 7 minute read

Supermodel Bella Hadid is widely renowned for her fashion style both on and off the runway. Ever since Bella's runway debut walking for Desigual during New York Fashion Week in 2014, the 25-year-old model has made a name for herself in the fashion world, earning her many fans. Because of her involvement with modeling for now-regarded nostalgic brands like Tommy Hilfiger and Marc Jacobs and her chic off-duty street style, a lot of the younger generation, specifically Gen Z, has hailed her as a fashion icon.

Over the years, many of Bella Hadid’s outfits have managed to look timelessly fashionable. In fact, there are many looks from Bella Hadid that one could take style inspiration from and recreate in a matter of minutes!

10 Bella's Off-Duty Look During 2022 New York Fashion Week

Bella’s white tank top and wide-leg shorts combo she showcased in this TikTok is a great place to start for an outfit recreation. The supermodel would later be seen wearing this street style ensemble out and about during 2022 New York Fashion Week, as reported by W Magazine, with a black button-up and purse added to the outfit. Simple closet essentials are all anyone needs to recreate this look.

9 Her 90s-Inspired Denim-On-Denim Street Style

The denim-on-denim look was all the rage in the 90s with popular celebrities of the time like Britney Spears and TLC embracing the monochromatic layering fashion. With Y2K coming back in style, it’s evident that Bella may have turned to that era for some fashion inspiration. In her early September 2022 Instagram pic, which she wore to the U.S. Open Tennis Championships in August according to reporting from Grazia, Bella wore a corset dark wash denim top, cuffed low-rise baggy jeans of the same color, and some black and white sneakers.

Related: Bella Hadid’s 10 Best Instagram Outfits

Since we’re guessing you have at least one denim type of clothing in your closet, this Bella Hadid 90s style can be easily recreated with items you already own. Try pairing up your own dark-wash jeans with a denim jacket of the same color. Or swap it out for a denim crop top if you have one. And if you’re dying to get a top similar to what Bella has, Zara's Denim Corset Top is a pretty good match!

8 Bella's Totally Tubular 80s-Inspired Dress And Leg Warmers

The 90s isn’t the only decade Bella Hadid seems to take inspiration from! Bella showcased her 80s-inspired outfit on her Instagram in early September 2022, taking various pictures posing in a high-neck striped dress and pink leg warmers. Of course, Bella modernized the look to fit her street style, as she wore some square shades and bright blue pumps to go along with it.

Related: Bella Hadid's 10 Best Street Fashion Looks

In case you are wondering, Bella's particular dress is from Chanel, which unfortunately seems to be sold out on their official website. Fortunately, however, ASOS DESIGN's Knitted Mini Dress In Multi Stripe is pretty similar to Bella’s dress. And as for the rest of the outfit recreation, all you need is some legwarmers—which you can easily find on Amazon—and heels.

7 Get Biker Chic With Bella's All Leather Outfit She Wore At Fendi's After Party

If you’re looking for an edgier outfit from Bella’s to recreate, her all-black and leather outfit she wore to Fendi’s after party during 2022 New York Fashion Week might be right up your alley. Wearing a shrug, corset, and pleated skirt, the model strutted into the event in footwear which looked to be a hybrid of knee-high boots and Nike sneakers. Designed by Ancute Sarca, the shoe was arguably one of the most eye-catching parts of Bella’s outfit. If you’re looking to get the shoe for your outfit recreation, a similar style by the same designer can be found online at Harvey Nicolas.

6 Or Cottagecore, Like Bella's Outfit She Wore In Holland

Cottagecore has truly become hallmarked as one of the popular fashion aesthetics of 2022 and with that came the return of the corset—one of Bella’s signature clothing pieces next to baggy jeans and sneakers. In a late August 2022 Instagram carousel, Bella was wandering the streets of Holland wearing a blush pink off-the-shoulder top under a white corset accented with pink ribbons over, classic light-washed baggy pants, and squared-toed high heels with socks. Bella completed the look with a black cross bag with metallic holes leading down its strap.

Since corsets are in again, which according to The New York Times credits to their design being more wearable and less costume-y part of the reason, you probably have a pretty high chance of finding one in order to recreate this whimsical Bella Hadid look. Micas' Lace Seam Detail Corset Top gives off the same cottagecore vibes as Bella’s. The rest of the recreation only needs some jeans, black heels, and a long-sleeve shirt to layer under the corset.

5 Try Making A Statement Like Bella With Her Silk Bandana Dress

For the celebration of the launching of her beverage brand Kin Euphorics, Bella dressed to impress by wearing a silky pink floral halter dress that looked reminiscent of a bandana. The Stone Bandana Minio-Dress from Hippie Vibe Tribe is somewhat similar in style to Bella's dress in terms of color and fit. Though if you have a favorite floral dress of your own, that could also work just as well for this Bella Hadid-inspired look.

4 Bella's Paris Outfit Also Makes For The Perfect Back-To-School Fit

This collared sweater and plaid skirt combo Bella wore during her trip to Paris is not only a good look to recreate, but is a great fall fashion inspo for back-to-school! All you need is a plaid skirt, a sweatshirt, and some sneakers. Add in any ol' purse, and you're good to go!

3 Get Pretty 'N' Punk Recreating One Of Bella's 2022 Paris Fashion Week Looks

While Bella is known for her Y2K-inspired looks, that doesn't mean she never drips her toes now and then into a different style aesthetic. Bella's look during the 2022 Paris Fashion Week gave off some punk rock vibes! In her mid-March 2022 Instagram carousel, one of the outfits Bella wore consisted of a high-neck white t-shirt layered under a black off-the-shoulder puff-sleeve top, and a purple and green plaid skirt held up by a leather belt with a pocket and some silver chains. Keeping up with the punk-inspired look, Bella finished off her outfit by wearing some chunky leather black boots.

If you're into more punk fashion, this would be an especially fun look to recreate. A lot of Bella’s look comes from layering clothes, so this would also make for an excellent outfit for the colder months.

2 For A Timeless Style, Try A Lil' Black Dress Similar To Bella's 2022 Met Gala's Ensemble

Bella's model-off-duty looks aren't the only thing that makes fans see her as fashion-forward! When it comes to red carpet events, Bella's graceful ensembles are known to grab some eyes, such as with her stunning black-and-white Jean Paul Gaultier Couture dress she wore to the Cannes red carpet look in July 2021. And her 2022 Met Gala look was no exception!

Designed by Burberry, the all-black ensemble Bella wore was made up of a custom-made corset bodysuit, an elegant trailed lace skirt with some tights and gloves to match, and strap heels. A chunky pearl ankle necklace also added to the elegance of Bella’s outfit. Because of how grand Bella’s 2022 Met Gala look is, this outfit would be great to recreate for a party dinner or a prestige event, especially since all you really need is a little black dress!

1 Can Never Go Wrong With Taking Inspo From Bella's Classic Y2K Style

Last but not least, we have Bella Hadid in one of her signature Y2K-inspired looks! A perfect outfit to recreate. Not to mention colorful too with her red and pink color-blocked cardigan, graphic tee, and denim and multicolored silk fused pleated skirt.

To recreate this look from Bella, you can layer a pleated denim skirt over a ruffled skirt like ASOS DESIGN's Ruffle Mini Skirt In Mix And Match Floral Print. And don't forget the boots and any favorite colorful tote bag or purse you have.

For more fashion inspiration from Bella Hadid, be sure to take a look at all the outfits she modeled for the 2023 Paris Fashion Show and follow her on Instagram and TikTok.
