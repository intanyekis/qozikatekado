Gal Gadot is a well-known Hollywood actress born April 30, 1985, in Petah Tikva, Israel. Gadot spent two years in the Israel Defense Forces before pursuing a career in acting. Her acting career began in Israeli television and film before she rose to international scenes in the DC Extended Universe films. Gadot is best known for her roles in blockbuster films such as Wonder Woman, Batman v Superman: Dawn of Justice, and the Fast and Furious franchise.

Gal has won the hearts of millions of fans worldwide with her stunning looks and powerful performances. Gadot has received numerous accolades for acting, including the Critics' Choice Award for Best Actress in an Action Movie for her role in Wonder Woman. Join us as we examine Gal Gadot's top 10 highest-grossing movies, whether you're a devoted fan or just interested in her career.

Keeping Up with the Joneses (2016) - $29.9 million

Greg Mottola, the movie's director, stars Gal Gadot, Isla Fisher, Jon Hamm, and Zach Galifianakis. Even though the film had a lot of big names in it, it only made $29.9 million at the worldwide box office, making it the least-profitable movie on this list. Even though it didn't do well at the box office, Keeping Up with the Joneses showed how versatile Gal Gadot is as an actress. It showed that she could hold her own in a comedic role with some of the biggest names in Hollywood.

Criminal (2016) - $38.8 million

Gadot co-stars in the 2016 action thriller with Tommy Lee Jones, Gary Oldman, and Kevin Costner. The movie centers on a death row inmate who receives a CIA agent's implanted memories to foil a dangerous terrorist plot. The criminal film failed to meet expectations in terms of box office success, earning only $38.8 million worldwide against a $31.5 million budget. Gadot's performance in the movie received positive reviews from critics, solidifying her position as a rising star in Hollywood.

Wonder Woman 1984 (2020) - $166.5 million

Gal Gadot's latest film, Wonder Woman 1984, comes in at number eight on our list, grossing $166.5 million worldwide. The film is a sequel to the hugely successful Wonder Woman, in which Gal plays Diana Prince, aka Wonder Woman. Set in the 1980s, the film pits Diana against two formidable foes, Maxwell Lord and Cheetah, as she attempts to avert a global disaster. The movie cemented Gal's place as a leading lady in Hollywood, which attracted audiences worldwide despite the critics' varying assessments.

Ralph Breaks the Internet (2018) - $529.3 million

Ralph Breaks the Internet is the seventh-highest-grossing Gal Gadot film. Gal voices Shank, a tough and talented online racing game driver, in this 2018 Walt Disney Animation Studios animated comedy. Ralph and Vanellope navigate the unpredictable internet in the film. Ralph Breaks the Internet made $529.3 million worldwide and established Gal as a versatile actor who can succeed in any genre.

Fast Five (2011) - $626.1 million

Gal became an action star after the 2011 film grossed $626.1 million worldwide. Gal plays Gisele Yashar, a former Mossad agent on Dominic Toretto's team, in Fast Five. Her tough, skilled fighter character helps the team rob Rio de Janeiro. Critics and audiences lauded Gal's performance, launching her Hollywood career. Fast Five is a thrilling action film that showcases Gal Gadot's talents and sets her up for future film success.

Justice League (2017) - $657.9 million

Justice League is a superhero film released in 2017. The film is based on the DC Comics superhero team of the same name and is directed by Zack Snyder, with Joss Whedon taking over for re-shoots and post-production. This film received mixed reviews from critics and audiences, with some praising the action sequences and performances while others criticized the plot, pacing, and visual effects. The film's box office performance was also underwhelming, leading to significant changes in the DC Extended Universe's future plans.

 Fast & Furious 6 (2013) - $788.7 million

The 2013 action-thriller Fast & Furious 6, directed by Justin Lin, is fourth on our list of the highest-grossing Gal Gadot films. In the movie, Gadot reprise her role as Gisele Yashar, a competent driver, and Dominic Toretto's team member. Fast & Furious 6 grossed $788.7 million at the global box office, which is a lot of money. This film also received favorable reviews from critics. Several of them enjoyed the action scenes and the acting, particularly Gal Gadot's portrayal of Gisele.

Wonder Woman (2017) - $821.8 million

Wonder Woman is Gal Gadot's third-highest-grossing film. Patty Jenkins directs Gal Gadot as the Amazon warrior who fights in World War I. Wonder Woman grossed over $821 million worldwide and was lauded for her feminist superhero representation. Gal Gadot's portrayal of the title character earned her Hollywood stardom. Wonder Woman 1984, in our list, followed Wonder Woman's triumph.

Batman v Superman: Dawn of Justice (2016) - $873.6 million

In an epic superhero crossover, Gal Gadot debuted as Wonder Woman alongside Ben Affleck's Batman and Henry Cavill's Superman. Despite conflicting reviews, the film was a commercial and critical success. One of the highest-grossing superhero films, it earned $873.6 million worldwide. Gadot's Wonder Woman, acclaimed for her strength, elegance, and beauty, laid the stage for her 2017 standalone picture. Batman v Superman: Dawn of Justice confirmed Gal Gadot's Hollywood stardom and heroic icon status.

Furious 7 (2015) - $1.516 billion

Furious 7 is Gal Gadot's most successful film. In this Fast and Furious film, Gal Gadot portrays former Mossad spy Gisele Yashar, who joins Dominic Toretto's team. Fast 7 grossed over $1.5 billion worldwide. Critics and fans praised the film's action, emotional depth, and moving memorial to Paul Walker, who died in 2013. Gal Gadot's performance as Gisele Yashar in Furious 7 established her as a rising Hollywood star.
