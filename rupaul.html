Who is RuPaul?

RuPaul Andre Charles is a famous American drag queen, actor, model, singer, television personality, and author who goes by RuPaul, born November 17, 1960.

RuPaul has produced and hosted RuPaul’s Drag Race since 2009, for which he has won eleven Primetime Emmy Awards, becoming the most-awarded person of colour in the show’s history. He is often regarded as America’s most commercially successful drag queen. He was named to the annual Time 100 list of the world’s most influential people in 2017. He was named “probably the world’s most renowned” drag queen by Fortune in 2019.

Biography TimeLine

RuPaul is an American drag queen, actor, singer, and television personality. He is best known for his work as a drag queen performer, host, and executive producer of the reality competition show RuPaul’s Drag Race. Born in San Diego, California, in 1960, RuPaul has become a prominent figure in LGBTQ+ culture and a trailblazer for the drag community. Here is a detailed biography timeline of his life:

Early Life

RuPaul was born to divorced parents in California when he was seven. When he was 15 and enrolled in a performing arts high school in Atlanta, Georgia, he moved in with one of his elder sisters. He began acting in drag while working in Atlanta’s underground film and nightclub scene, where he had always enjoyed dressing up. In 1987, he travelled to New York City and started his career in go-go bars and MTV’s The Gong Show.

Career

In 1993, he released Supermodel of the World, a dance-pop album that included the uplifting track “Supermodel (You Better Work).” The song became an unexpected mainstream smash thanks to a music video where he strutted in a blonde wig and many attractive clothes. RuPaul was cast in several films as a result of the show’s success, including Crooklyn (1994), Blue in the Face (1995), The Brady Bunch Movie (1995), and the drag-themed comedy To Wong Foo, Thanks for Everything, Julie Newmar (1995). Personal life

In 1994, at the Limelight nightclub in New York City, RuPaul met painter Georges LeBar.

They tied the knot in January 2017. RuPaul has stated that he would not want to “place limitations” on the person he loves; thus, they have an open marriage. The couple lives in Los Angeles on a 60,000-acre (24,000-hectare) ranch in Wyoming.

Environmentalists chastised the couple in 2020 after RuPaul revealed that they lease mineral rights and sell water to oil firms on their farm and enable fracking, a dangerous practice to the environment. According to public maps, the ranch has at least 35 operating wells.
