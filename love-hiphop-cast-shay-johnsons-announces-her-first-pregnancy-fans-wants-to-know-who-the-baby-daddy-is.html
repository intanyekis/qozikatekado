Shay Johnson, one of the cast members of “Love & Hip Hop: Miami” is pregnant and fans are curious to know who is the baby daddy.

Love & Hip Hop: Miami is a spin-off of the original Love & Hip Hop TV franchise. The show premiered on January 1, 2018.

Who Is ‘Love & HipHop’ Cast Shay Johnson’s Baby Daddy?

“Love & HipHop” cast Shay Johnson’s baby daddy is not yet revealed is Bambi’s husband Scrappy according to the fans.

The fans have speculated that the baby daddy is Lil Scrappy, birth name Darryl Raynard Richardson III. Scrappy is the ex-boyfriend of Shay and is now married to Bambi.

Scrappy’s mom, Momma Dee made an appearance at Shay’s baby shower which only fueled the rumors about Scrappy being the baby daddy.

Some of the fans commented, “Who is the Papi?”, “It is giving Scrappy Vibes because Momma dee looks like a proud granny.”

The news of Shay getting pregnant spread like a fire on Twitter too. Fans are tweeting about her pregnancy.

Shay Johnson Pregnancy Announcement

Shay Johnson made a pregnancy announcement via an Instagram post. The star kept this secret for months but announced the pregnancy news after posting stunning pictures of her growing belly bump for the cover of Kontrol Magazine.

For so long Johnson has wanted a baby and it is finally happening.  On the other picture of Shay showing her belly bump, she revealed the gender of the baby. She captioned the picture “ITS A GIRL! I am going to be a mommy. So excited!!”

Shay Johnson New Boyfriend Alert!

Shay Johnson is reported to be single as she does not have a boyfriend at the moment. Previously, she dated Lil Scrappy for about 3 years.

The couple split and the reasons for their separation remain unknown. Shay is now single again meanwhile Scrappy is married.
