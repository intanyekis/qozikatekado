About Maggie Haney

Maggie Haney was born on February 8, 1978, and is famous for coaching 2016 Olympic gold medalist Laurie Hernandez and 2018 World champion Riley McCusker.

Haney began her career as a gymnast at the age of three.

Haney began her coaching career in 2007, and from 2013 to 2020, she had at least one athlete from her program on the national team roster.

In the course of her career, Haney was suspended by the USA Gymnastics over half a dozen allegations bordering on verbal and emotional abuse.

Some of the complaints included Haney “instructing injured gymnasts to remove casts and continue training, yelling at and threatening gymnasts.”
