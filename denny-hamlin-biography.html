Who is Denny Hamlin?

Denny Hamlin is an American professional “Stock Car Racing” driver. He himself hardly ever refers to his real name as he prefers to call it “Denny.”

Denny Hamlin currently competes full-time in the NASCAR Sprint Cup Series, driving the No.11 Toyota Camry for Gibbs Racing, and part-time in the Xfinity Series, driving the No. 18 Toyota Camry for JGR.
