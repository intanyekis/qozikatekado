Kristin Cavallari appeared in the video mouthing to a clip that said "So what are you gonna do about it?" A voice responded with, "We're reporting it," to which Kristen said "Are you gonna arrest me? Are you gonna give me a ticket?"

Kristin's Priorities When It Comes To Relationships

When Kristin Cavallari announced she was dating 24-year-old influencer Mark Estes, it came as a shock to her fans, who weren't aware that she was dating anyone since her divorce from former NFL player Jay Cutler.

The divorce was reportedly amicable, and the two of them have a great co-parenting relationship.

"With great sadness, after 10 years together we have come to a loving conclusion to get a divorce. We have nothing but love and respect for one another and are deeply grateful for the years shared, memories made, and the children we are so proud of," she announced in 2020. "This is just the situation of two people growing apart."

Now, almost four years later, she's happy in a new relationship, while Jay Cutler has also found love again.

Kristin Cavallari and Mark Estes seem to be in a very good place, but the reality TV star has been very clear about who are her priorities, and that's her three children, not her partner.She is "first and foremost" a mother. Even so, she's glad to have some time for herself.

"I've had a lot of fun in the last few years, just really focusing on my friends and getting to hang out with my friends again, because I didn't get to do that for a long time," she said last year. "Really, when you're having little babies and three babies, essentially, at one point in time, that was my world. And so it's been nice to just have fun again."
