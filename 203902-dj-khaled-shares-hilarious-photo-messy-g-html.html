DJ Khaled Shares Hilarious Photo of Messy Grey Hair &amp; Beard Amid Quarantine

June 2024 · 2 minute read

Several celebrities have had a bit of a challenging time looking like their usual selves in this self-isolation period. DJ Khaled seems to fall into that category.

On the flip side, however, it showed what he looks like without any of that. The DJ explained that he’s now desperate to get a haircut, of course, while poking some fun at himself in the process.

DJ Khaled attends the City Of Hope's Spirit of Life 2019 Gala held at The Barker Hanger in Santa Monica, California on October 10, 2019. | Photo: Getty Images

Of course, he could choose to take matters into his own hands and give himself a trim.or have his wife give him the trim. However, Khaled appears to be dead set on getting a professional cut.

“I will get the haircut I will figure it out soon...Im get my Barbour a space suit stay tuned! I NEED MY BEARD ? OIL! Lol !”

Several of his friends and colleagues expressed how relatable the situation is in the comment section. Some even explained that they’re all in the same boat.

However, a lot of fans also had a field day laughing at the DJ, with some pointing out how normal he looks now.

Several other celebrities appear to have figured out the haircut problem.

People immediately noted his haircut, with even celebrities commenting on how weird he looks without his usual trim. Fat Joe, another hip-hop artist, said at the time:

“I’m a come threw give u a cut lol.”

While Khaled seems to be working on getting his barber, however, several other celebrities appear to have figured out the haircut problem.
