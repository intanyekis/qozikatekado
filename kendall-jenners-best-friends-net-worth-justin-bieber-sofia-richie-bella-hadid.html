10 Of Kendall Jenners Closest Friends, Ranked By Net Worth

July 2024 · 6 minute read

This list is all about model Kendall Jenner and her ultra-rich friends. The famous reality television star with an estimated net worth of $45 million certainly likes hanging out with other rich and beautiful people and this article will be ranking them by their net worth.

Related: Ranking Kylie Jenner’s Best Friends By Net Worth

As fans already know, Kendall has plenty of close friends in the modeling industry such as Hailey Baldwin and Gigi Hadid but the top two celebs on this list may surprise some as one of them is one of Kendall's ex-boyfriends, and the other she was often rumored to be more than just friends with. But alright, without spoiling too much, here they are — 10 of Kendall Jenner’s closest friends, ranked by their net worth!

10 Fai Khadra — Net Worth $1.3 Million

Kicking the list off is model Fai Khadra. Recently, the 28-year old was spotted spending quite a bit of time with Kendall Jenner — he even went on vacation to Utah with her, Kylie and some of their friends — and every time Kendall is asked about him she was adamant that they are just friends.

Fai is definitely not a staple name in the world of the rich and famous yet which is why his net worth of $1.3 million put him on spot number 10 on this list!

9 Willow Smith — Net Worth $6 Million

Next on the list is singer Willow Smith who has known Kendall Jenner for quite some time now as both of them had pretty luxurious upbringings and were always running into each other in the same social circle.

Above is a photo of the two ladies hanging out at Coachella in 2014 and while they may not be hanging out every day they are still occasionally seen catching up. According to Celebrity Net Worth, Willow Smith is estimated to have a net worth of around $6 million.

8 Jaden Smith — Net Worth $8 Million

Speaking of Willow Smith, her older brother Jaden is also very close to the Jenners and back in 2013 Jaden even briefly dated Kendall’s younger sister Kylie Jenner. Even though that relationship didn’t last for too long, it seems as if the actor remained close to both sisters and paparazzi frequently catch them hanging out.

7 Sofia Richie — Net Worth $8 Million

Next on the list is yet another friend that Kendall Jenner has been closed for quite a few years. Yes, model Sofia Richie also grew up in the same social circles as Kendall, so it doesn’t come as a surprise that the two ladies became close over the years.

According to Celebrity Net Worth, Sofia Richie is estimated to have a net worth of around $8 million which actually means that she is tied with Jaden Smith on this list.

6 Hailey Baldwin — Net Worth $20 Million

As everyone will quickly notice, a lot of the friends on this list are friends that Kendall Jenner has been close to since her teens. Fellow model Hailey Baldwin is certainly one of those and over the years the two not only bonded over living the same lifestyle but over their successful modeling careers as well.

According to Celebrity Net Worth, Hailey Baldwin currently has an estimated net worth of around $20 million which puts her on spot number six on this list.

5 Bella Hadid — Net Worth $25 Million

Opening up the top five richest friends of Kendall Jenner is yet another model — this time we're talking about Bella Hadid. It is certainly no secret that Bella and Kendall grew super close over the years and they were even frequently seen going on vacation with each other.

In the photo above, the two ladies are spending some quality time together at an NBA basketball game. Currently, Bella Hadid is estimated to have a net worth of around $25 million.

4 Cara Delevingne — Net Worth $28 Million

Who says that models are always each other’s competition — anyone who takes a look at Kendall Jenner’s friends will quickly realize that a majority of them are fellow models, and Cara Delevingne is one of them.

3 Gigi Hadid — Net Worth $29 Million

Opening up the top three richest friends of Kendall Jenner is the last model on today’s list — Gigi Hadid. Gigi and Kendall had their breakthrough in the modeling industry around the same time and both of them are currently as successful as ever.
