Hope Confesses Her Feelings for Finn

July 2024 · 2 minute read

On the heels of Hope’s fantasy kiss with Finn comes a bombshell confession that could change everything at Forrester Creations if one Bold & Beautiful character sees the writing on the wall!

After decades of fighting over Liam, it seemed Hope and Steffy had moved on from loving the same man, but Hope’s bodice-ripping daydream about Steffy’s husband Finn proves that may not be true at all.

Hope was resolute that after leaving Liam and experiencing what it was like to have the love of a man who was solely devoted to her, she wouldn’t go back, but her heart, or at the very least her libido, didn’t get the memo.

After multiple massage sessions with Finn and his talented hands, it’s clear that if Hope got a chance to hit the sheets with Dr. Feelgood, she wouldn’t be offering up her migraine headaches as an excuse. Obviously, she’ll try to resist the forbidden feelings; who would willingly walk back into that particular type of lion’s den? But, as they say, the heart wants what it wants, and it may become too much to deny.

In fact, Bold & Beautiful spoilers tell us Hope will make a bombshell confession in the coming days, and that can only mean she’ll confide in someone about her growing feelings for Finn. That someone? Her mother, Brooke.

Now, Brooke is nothing if not uber-experienced in these kinds of messy entanglements, so one would expect her to see the writing on the wall right away. With Hope’s line in a precarious position at Forrester Creations, any whiff of her daughter wanting Steffy’s husband will launch her out of the frying pan and straight into the proverbial fire!

We fully expect this to trigger Brooke’s protective instincts, which may lead her to do something that will change everything at Forrester.

What will she do? Why, she’ll reconsider and accept Ridge’s offer to take on the duties of co-CEO alongside Steffy… in order to protect Hope from the fallout that is undoubtedly headed her way thanks to this new development.

We were all about Steffy moving on from the dreaded triangle with Finn, but it looks like she’s doomed to land back in the same situation repeatedly. It all sounds like a lot of drama, chaos, not to mention deja vu to us!
